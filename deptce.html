Introduction

The department of Civil Engineering was established during the year 1980 with an intake of 40 and it was accredited to NBA during the year 2006 and then to AICTE during the year 1994 till now. the department is supported with well qualified staff members in almost all the specialization of Civil Engineering. presently the intake of UG program has been enhanced to 120. further the department is also provided with MTech program in Transportation Engineering and Systems with an intake of 18. The department is also supported with the research center (Ph.D and MSc) recognised by VTU.

We are proud to inform that the department is succeeded in placing the UG final year students in different reputed construction companies. The department is also active with the MoU’s from different construction industries and conducting seminars and workshops more regularly.

We are proud to state that the department of Civil Engineering undertakes consultancy services both from government and private organizations and generates the revenue to the extent of Rs.20 to Rs.25 lakh per year .


Vision

  • To become a centre of academic excellence and place for learning professional skills to cater the technical challenges posed by the ever changing global environment and development.

Mission

  • To impart quality education and advances in Civil Engineering to meet the changing global scenario.
  • To assimilate the spirit of creativity, management and entrepreneurship in Civil Engineering and interdisciplinary field with ethical values
  • To provide a healthy ambience for teaching, research, consultancy and industry interaction activities to solve engineering problems

# Program Educational Objectives
Graduates will be able to:
PEO - 1 Pursue Civil Engineering profession as competent professionals working in private and government sectors by providing technically, economically and socially acceptable solutions.
PEO - 2 Pursue life-long learning in their profession by developing technical expertise in the use of modern tools and higher education leading to improved professional expertise and excel in teaching profession.
PEO - 3 Exhibit good communication skills while working as team members and team leaders and also possess team spirit and leadership qualities.
PEO - 4 Follow established professionalism, ethics, public policy, safety and sustainability as professional leaders and contributors to society when solving engineering problems and providing solutions.

# Program Specific Outcomes
PSO - 1 Plan, analyze, design and execute cost effective Civil Engineering problems to meet the societal needs.
PSO - 2 Learn advance techniques in the state of the art Civil Engineering including interdisciplinary techniques and develop new solutions to critical problems without compromising the standards.

# Program Outcomes
PO - 1 Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
PO - 2 Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.
PO - 3 Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.
PO - 4 Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions.
PO - 5 Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
PO - 6 Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice.
PO - 7 Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development.
PO - 8 Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of the engineering practice.
PO - 9 Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.
PO - 10 Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.
PO - 11 Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.
PO - 12 Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.

Department Advisory Board/Committee

The department Academic advisory committee comprises HOD, Senior faculty, two external academician, two members from industry, two alumni members and two student members. The committee will review the academic quality and suggest improvements in the related areas.

Details on Lab facilities

The Department of Civil Engineering infrastructure is comprised of number of class rooms, seminar hall and campus green spaces. The department has well spaced rooms with facilities of CCTV, Black board and a projector.

Name of the Laboratory Name of the Important Equipment
Basic Material Testing Laboratory Universal Testing Machine, Brinells Hardness Testing Machine, Torsion Testing Machine, Compression Testing Machine, Loading Frame
Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory Triaxial test apparatus, Shear test apparatus, Standard Penetration Test
Highway and Concrete Laboratory Benkelmen Beam, Ductility Testing Machine, Soundness Testing Machine, CBR Testing Machine, Aggregate Impact Testing Machine
Hydraulics Engineering Laboratory Venturi flume, Pelton Wheel Turbine, Kaplan Turbine Francis Turbine, Bernoulli’s Equipment
Environmental Engineering Laboratory Spectrophotometer, Flame photometer, Colorimeter pH Meter, BOD Incubator, Nephelo Turbidity Meter
Surveying Laboratory Total Station, Theodolite, Dumpy Level, Magnetic Compass
CADD Laboratory Computers Softwares



Additional Lab Facilities

Facility Name Details
STADD Pro Structural analysis and design software.
M X Roads Software used for accurate and rapid road design.

Class room

Department has six spacious class rooms with adequate seating capacity. Each class room is provided with Computer system, LCD Projector with screen facility. In addition to that Green glass board and Podium are provided in each class rooms


Department Library
The department has well maintained library with around 1400 books. The lists of previous year’s projects are made available in the department library with project reports. Along with Laboratory Manuals, Journals / Magazines, Data Sheets, Power Point Presentation, handouts, Course notes, Bound Volumes, Project Reports, conference proceedings and IS Code books are made available in the Department Library.

Seminar/Auditorium facility
Department has an air conditioned seminar hall with about 120 seating capacity. It is acoustically designed for good audio listening.

Research Initiatives

VTU Research Centre in the Department of Civil Engineering was started in the year 2002, with provision for Ph.D. (Full Time / Part Time), Integrated Ph.D. and M.Sc. (Engg.). Since then the research facility is available for research scholars to carry out research in their specific area. Students and research scholars are encouraged to take up inter disciplinary research in various domains like Transportation Engineering, Highway Engineering, Hydraulics and Water Resource Management, Remote Sensing and GIS, Soft computing, etc

The history of JNN College of Engineering is marked by events that have made it synonymous with quality education. Established in 1980 by NES, the College is affiliated to VTU, Belagavi and is recognized by the AICTE and Government of Karnataka.
