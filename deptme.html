MECHANICAL ENGINEERING


Introduction

Established in 1980, Mechanical Engineering department has a dynamic, highly qualified & experienced faculty constantly engaged in R&D activities. Fully equipped labs with CAD/CAM/CAE, CNC training and Robot facilities and Condition Monitoring lab provide an environment where students can experience the vast expanse of Mechanical Engineering. The department has a number of research projects in the areas of stress analysis, vibration, composite materials sponsored by AICTE/VTU. The department also offers M.Tech Degrees in Design Engineering and Computer Integrated Manufacturing and has been recognized as a research center by VTU, leading to Doctoral and M.Sc.(Engg.) degrees.

Vision

  • To be a model department with academic excellence and technical expertise, grooming students to become Mechanical Engineers of best quality to meet ever changing needs of industry and society.

Mission

  • Impart technical education of highest standard by qualified and experienced faculty in the field of Mechanical Engineering through effective teaching learning process.

  • Continuously upgrade knowledge, skill and engage in Research & Development activity by executing challenging projects in association with academia and industry.

  • Imbibe ethical, environmental and social values amongst students using value based education.

# Program Educational Objectives
PEO - 1 Apply the knowledge of Mechanical Engineering Sciences to design and develop products, processes to meet the needs of the industry and society.
PEO - 2 Work individually / in groups with integrity, ethical and environmental standards.
PEO - 3 Pursue higher studies; upgrade knowledge of scientific and technological developments, engage in research and also to become an entrepreneur.

# Program Specific Outcomes
PSO - 1 Utilize the knowledge of mechanics, materials, design, thermal and production engineering to fulfil the requirements of industry.
PSO - 2 Carry out development projects on mechatronic systems, industrial and agricultural related machineries, composite materials and bio-fuels for sustainable needs.
PSO - 3 Work individually and in team to develop new products and processes by using modern machine tools and software.

# Program Outcomes
PO - 1 Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
PO - 2 Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.
PO - 3 Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.
PO - 4 Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions.
PO - 5 Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
PO - 6 Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice.
PO - 7 Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development.
PO - 8 Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of the engineering practice.
PO - 9 Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.
PO - 10 Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.
PO - 11 Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.
PO - 12 Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.

Research Initiatives

VTU Research Centre in the Department of Mechanical Engineering was started in the year 2001, with provision for Ph.D. (Full Time / Part Time), Integrated Ph.D. and M.Sc. (Engg.). Since then the research facility is available for research scholars to carry out research in their specific area. Students and research scholars are encouraged to take up inter disciplinary research in various domains like Design, Thermal, Materials, etc. Student innovationcenter is open 24X7 to carry out innovative projects. 25% of faculty members are guiding Ph.D. students in various fields of Mechanical Engineering. Open Seminars for Research Scholars will be conducted at regular intervals to assess their Research progress. Department of Mechanical Engineering is engaged in developing state of the art technologies in collaboration with private/public sector organizations.

Innovations by the Faculty in Teaching and Learning

  • Faculty members provide quality study material to enrich students learning.
  • Mode of teaching in this Institute is not only limited to the traditional chalk and talk methods but also an amalgamation of modern technology (Ex. Power point presentation, audio video teaching, demonstration of models etc.)
  • Students are encouraged to find topics pertaining to course. These topics are presented by the students to peers. The contents and the presentations quality are assessed and considered while awarding CIE marks.
  • Students are encouraged to visit NPTEL lecturers, browse different internet sites to increase their knowledge base about the subject. Moreover through these activities, students acquire relevant knowledge which is beyond the syllabus as per the university curriculum
  • The technical fest “TECHZONE” organized by the college also serves to create opportunities for the students self development based on extra syllabus technological knowhow.
  • Faculty members arrange various learning programs like quiz, debate, seminars etc., and encourage students to participate.
  • Faculty frequently arranges industrial and site visits to expose the students to real world environment.
