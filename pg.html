Seat Matrix:

  • 80 % of the seats will be filled by Government of Karnataka through Karnataka Examination Authority with PGCET ranking
  • 20% seats through Management quota

      Duration:

  • 2 years (4 Semesters)

      Eligibility:

  • Students who have passed B.E./B.Tech. in respective branch as per VTU and KEA with at least 50% of marks in the above subjects taken together.The details of Admission of Government seats is available at http://kea.kar.nic.in
  • For admission under management quota, students should compulsorily appeared in PGCET/GATE or any other entrance test for professional program.

 PGCET:

  • College other Fee is Rs. 25,000/- (Annually)
  • Extra transport charge of Rs 3,000/-( payable annually) for Bhadravathi & Nyamathi Students.

About Us

The history of JNN College of Engineering is marked by events that have made it synonymous with quality education. Established in 1980 by NES, the College is affiliated to VTU, Belagavi and is recognized by the AICTE and Government of Karnataka.
